The Crustburger

Description

The Crustburger is a delicious burger featuring a crispy, caramelized crust on the beef patty, topped with melted cheese, fresh lettuce, tomato, and your favorite condiments, all served on a toasted bun. The secret is in the seared crust that locks in flavor and juiciness.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 recommended)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise (optional)

Steps

  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape into patties, slightly larger than the buns. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a cast-iron skillet or heavy pan over high heat until very hot. Do not add oil.
  3. Place the patties in the skillet and press down gently. Cook without moving for 3-4 minutes, until a deep brown crust forms.
  4. Flip the patties, add a slice of cheese to each, and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the cheese melts and the burgers are cooked to your liking.
  5. Meanwhile, butter the buns and toast them in a separate pan or under a broiler until golden.
  6. Assemble the burgers: place lettuce, tomato, and pickles on the bottom bun, add the crusted patty with cheese, and top with your favorite condiments and the top bun.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
