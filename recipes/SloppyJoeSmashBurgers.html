Sloppy Joe Smash Burgers

Description

Sloppy Joe Smash Burgers combine the classic flavors of a Sloppy Joe with the crispy, caramelized edges of a smash burger. Each burger is topped with a savory, tangy Sloppy Joe sauce and melty cheese, served on a toasted bun for a deliciously messy meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Butter for toasting buns

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper, cooking until softened. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute more.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, and mustard. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until thickened. Set aside.
  3. Divide ground beef into 4 balls. Heat a large skillet or griddle over high heat. Place beef balls on the hot surface and smash flat with a spatula. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes until a crust forms, then flip. Top each patty with a spoonful of Sloppy Joe sauce and a slice of cheese. Cook until cheese melts.
  5. Butter and toast the buns.
  6. Assemble burgers: place patties on buns and top with extra Sloppy Joe sauce if desired. Serve immediately.
